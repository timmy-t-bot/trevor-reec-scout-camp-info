How to Work on Merit Badges

  • Contact your merit badge counselor before camp to understand requirements
  • Bring any partially completed merit badges with proper documentation
  • Attend scheduled merit badge sessions during the week
  • Complete requirements during free time and activity periods
  • Have your counselor sign off on completed requirements before leaving camp

Important Information

  • Check with your troop leaders for specific merit badge counselors assigned to your scouts
  • Some merit badges may require additional materials or prerequisites
  • Week 2 typically offers more advanced merit badge opportunities than week 1
  • Special guest instructors may be available for certain merit badges
  • Bring completed Blue Cards for any merit badges you're working on

Recommendations

  • Contact the camp directly or your troop leadership for specific merit badge offerings
  • Review merit badge requirements in advance to be prepared
  • Bring necessary supplies for any merit badges you plan to work on
